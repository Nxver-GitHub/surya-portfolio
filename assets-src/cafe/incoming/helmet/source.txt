Modifications (by this project)
-------------------------------
- Rescaled to real-world modern F1 helmet dimensions (0.30 m front-to-back
  including rear spoiler), base of the shell grounded at Z=0, origin at
  footprint center, visor oriented along -X (facing into the room when
  shelf-mounted).
- Full livery repaint — "Senna-inspired homage": the entire original Zhou
  Guanyu 2024 design was stripped and replaced. Removed EVERY real-world mark:
  the "周冠宇" (ZHOU) name characters, the white city-skyline sponsor blocks,
  the green chevron/arrow bands, the purple crest/ornament, the "Speedhui /
  2024 / 上帝保佑" visor tear-off script and cloud motifs, and all remaining
  green text scripts. No text or logos remain anywhere on the model.
- Repainted in an unmistakable Senna colour language WITHOUT reproducing the
  exact registered stripe geometry (band widths and the crown sweep are
  varied so it reads as homage, not replica): bright yellow shell base, a
  green band sweeping the crown/brow and dipping forward toward the visor,
  blue accents around the visor surround / lower shell.
- Visor rebuilt as dark smoke (near-opaque, glossy clearcoat; the branded
  tear-off strip was discarded). Interior liner reset to a neutral matte
  dark fabric.
- Material corrections: the source used packed ORM / metallic-roughness
  textures split via Separate Color nodes; these were replaced entirely.
  Shell/trim now read as glossy automotive paint (metallic 0, roughness ~0.14,
  light clearcoat) baked from a procedural Senna colour field into WebP
  base-colour textures. Visor: metallic 0, roughness 0.06, IOR 1.45. No
  emission leaks.
- Decimated for the web (visor mesh was ~56k tris of a smooth lens; shell
  kept dense to preserve curvature normals) and compressed: meshopt geometry
  compression + WebP textures via @gltf-transform/cli (Draco not used).

Destination: GT Café display exhibit (public/models/cafe-helmet.glb)
