Modifications (by this project)
-------------------------------
- Rescaled to real-world Lancia Stratos HF dimensions (length 3.71 m), wheels
  grounded at Z=0, origin at footprint center, nose oriented along +X.
- Trademark scrub (public repo): removed all real-world marks — every "ALITALIA"
  wordmark, "LANCIA" text, "RALLYE MONTE CARLO" event plate and "1" badge, the
  driver-name scripts ("S. Munari / S. Maiga"), the "HF" shield badges, the
  "CAMPIONE DEL MONDO" caption, the "ROME" rally-plate labels, and the sponsor
  decals (Pirelli / Facom / Magneti / Champion / Campagnolo, etc.). The abstract
  green/white/red arrow livery geometry is retained as national racing colors,
  which carry no wordmarks. The main racing numbers were removed for a clean
  exhibit read.
- Material corrections: driven Principled sliders directly where the UE4-authored
  packed ORM/metallic-roughness channels misread — car paint reads as glossy
  paint (metallic 0, roughness ~0.05), tyres/mechanical retain their ORM maps,
  and the glass was rebuilt as proper glass (roughness 0.05, transmission 0.85,
  IOR 1.45). No emission leaks.
- Compressed for the web: meshopt geometry compression + WebP textures via
  @gltf-transform/cli (Draco not used).

Destination: GT Café display exhibit (public/models/cafe-stratos.glb)
